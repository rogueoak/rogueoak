Meet Thought Stream
Something new is taking root at Rogue Oak. Thought Stream is a hands-free way to capture your thinking out loud - on your iPhone and in CarPlay. Tap once, ask Siri, or press Start in the car, then just talk.
Ideas arrive when your hands are busy - driving, walking, cooking. Thought Stream keeps the friction near zero: speak, pause to think, and edit entirely by voice with a control word, so you never touch the screen. Speech-to-text runs on the device, so your words never leave your phone.
It is still in early development. Stay tuned - you will be among the first to know when it ships.
